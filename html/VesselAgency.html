Vessel Port Agency Services

We serve as the reliable, round-the-clock representative for ship owners and charterers at the Ports of Mombasa and Dar es Salaam. Our focus is on achieving the fastest possible vessel turnaround time (TAT).

Core Agency Functions

  • → Pre-arrival planning and coordination with port authorities.
  • → Crew change formalities, medical assistance, and repatriation.
  • → Handling cash-to-master (CTM) and bunker delivery arrangements.
  • → Coordination of cargo operations, loading, and discharging.

Efficiency & Cost Control

Our local expertise ensures compliance with all local regulations and port tariffs, preventing unnecessary delays or penalties. We provide transparent, detailed Disbursement Accounts (D/A) every time.
